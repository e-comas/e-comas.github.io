Our Expertise

With the e-Comas team by your side, you’ll have a dedicated, proficient and certified partner who knows retail media inside and out.

Strategy & Planning

Our ad specialists forecast ad sales, total sales and budget based on your current campaign performance. No data? No problem! We’ll use benchmarks to plan your campaigns.

Amazon PPC

Using the latest paid search strategies with a full funnel focus, we can help with an account audit, strategy, campaign setup and optimisation to drive growth and hit your targets.

Amazon DSP

Get ahead with DSP to grow your customer funnel from outside of Amazon. Mixed with AMC reports and audience builds, we ensure we can target precise and refined audiences for your brand.

AMC

Our Amazon Marketing Cloud-certified professionals leverage deep insights and make informed decisions, amplifying your success. Whether it's creating custom dashboards or navigating the sea of data, we provide insights that can transform your strategy.

AMS

We’ll help you maximize your advertising campaigns with real-time data from Amazon Marketing Stream to make the most of the high conversion hours of the day.

Amazon Attribution

Launch off-Amazon campaigns (Google Ads, Social Media Ads, email campaigns) to drive traffic to your Amazon listings – it’s great for organic ranking!

Google Ads

We can define your Google Ads strategy and manage Search, Display and Shopping Ads. Our team can also set up everything you need to succeed, like Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager.

Social Media Ads

Harnessing the power of social media sites, we help put your products in front of the people most likely to buy them, as well as building click-through traffic to boost your rankings.

Criteo & Citrus Ads

Let us audit your account, create a winning strategy and help you with your advertising campaign setup and optimisation to succeed on these two major retail media platforms. Analytics
